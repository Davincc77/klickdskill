## Instructions

**Add to your README if your project supports `.klickd v3.4`:**

1. Copy the Markdown snippet for the appropriate variant:
   - **Blue** — your project reads and writes valid `.klickd v3.4` files
   - **Green** — your project has been tested against the official benchmark suite
   - **Grey** — `.klickd` support is in progress or partial

2. Paste it at the top of your `README.md`, alongside your other status badges.

3. The badge links to the official `.klickd` spec repository at `github.com/Davincc77/klickdskill`.
